Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2016

Pharmacy Current is committed to the privacy and protection of our users and visitors. The following Privacy Policy outlines this site’s collection and treatment of our user’s information.

Collection of Information

By registering with Pharmacy Current on our Registration Page, users provide us with personally identifiable information including Name, Email address, Mailing Address, Phone Number, and other information related to the user’s interests and profession. By submitting this registration form, users agree that this information is not confidential and may be shared with Pharmacy Current’s partnering advertisers and other third parties.

Cookies

At the time of this writing, Pharmacy Current does not implement cookies to track user activity on our site. Advertisers and third parties linked to our site may use cookies and other methods to track activity. All users have the option to disable cookies on their computer by adjusting the settings on their web browser. There are also several methods available to remove existing cookies on their device. See your settings menu for more information.

Security

Pharmacy Current utilizes multiple security features to maintain the privacy of our users and visitors. Pharmacy Current is not responsible or liable in the event that your information is intercepted, stolen, accessed, or distributed by any third party in a manner which you have not given written consent. By visiting Pharmacy Current, you agree to assume all risk.

Unsubscribe and Opt-in/Opt-out Policy

By visiting our site and/or registering with Pharmacy Current, you are opting in to receive informational and promotional messages from Pharmacy Current and its partners. Pharmacy Current offers visitors to our web site and recipients of our marketing campaigns the opportunity to “opt-out” of receiving communications from us and from our marketing partners, advertisers, and other third parties. You can also request to be removed from our database at any time by emailing info@pharmacycurrent.com. Should an individual unsubscribe from our communications, Pharmacy Current will make every possible attempt to comply. However due to delays in processing, human error, or other uncontrollable circumstances, it is possible that you could continue to receive our communications. If you receive communications from Pharmacy Current after opting-out, please email us at info@pharmacycurrent.com. Please note that if you wish to stop receiving communications from any of our marketing partners, advertisers, or other third parties you must communicate with these entities directly. We are unable to process unsubscribe requests for any of our marketing partners, advertisers, or other third parties.

Users under the Age of 14:

Pharmacy Current is a website destination and resource for Pharmacy Professionals, and is not to be used by children under the age of 14. Visitors under the age of 14 are prohibited from registering with Pharmacy Current, and any registrant found to be under the age of 14 may be deleted from our database.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Pharmacy Current reserves the right to change the Privacy Policy at any time. In an effort to keep our visitors informed, Pharmacy Current will update the privacy policy to reflect any changes made to the way we collect, store, or share information. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the changes we make to the Privacy Policy. Any time an update is made to the Pharmacy Current Privacy Policy, the date will be noted at the top of the page for your information.

Thank you for your interest in Pharmacy Current. Questions and concerns can be sent to info@pharmacycurrent.com.
